Mac and Cheese In a Slow Cooker

The completed mac and cheese recipe

  • 1 16 ounce pacakge of elbow macaroni
  • Half a cup of butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 16 ounce package shreded cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1 5 ounce can of evaporated milk
  • 2 eggs, well beaten
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1 10.5 ounce can condensed cheddar cheese soup
  • 1 pinch of paprika or as desired (optional)
  1. Fill a large pot with light salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in macaroni and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasinally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain and transfer pasta to a slow cooker.
  2. Add butter to pasta and stir until melted, season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle about 1/2 of the Cheddar cheese over pasta and stir.
  3. Whisk evaporated milk and eggs together in a bowl until smooth; stir into pasta mixture
  4. Whisk milk and condesned soup tofether in a bowl until smooth; sir into pasta mixture.
  5. Sprinkle remaining cheese over pasta mixture; garnish with paprika.
